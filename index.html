i also want to clear up some things before they become an issues or concerns:

i draw angst, violence, blood, and the like because i was a warrior cats kid. ive basically been coded to draw my favorite guys being dramatic/fighting/exploding things.
its not because i have issues or condone real violence. please do not mix up artist/writer/actor and character :3

this being said, take the trigger warnings i put on stuff seriously.
if i say "yo theres gonna be some illustrated blood/death/drug reference/etc etc", you better believe it.
i like to keep my art mostly family friendly and silly, and there will be stuff for those who prefer fluff, but there are also darker themes! i do not censor, i only warn.

this does not mean i draw other more adult topics. i will never draw things that are suggestive or explicit. i am not only a minor but also that is a boundary that should not be violated no matter my age. do not ask me to draw suggestive/explicit content. i will block you.

saint really is a tribute to movement and the feeling of your first playthrough. you cant fight back (unless specific circumstances are met) and you are equipped with a grappling tongue. so many combinations can be made and will be made in the heat of the moment. the smooth swinging transitioning into a roll, then a pounce, and back into a swing again as you flee from a red lizard. nearly falling to your death to then, at the last second, attach to the nearest surface and be flung upwards. saint is momentum.
